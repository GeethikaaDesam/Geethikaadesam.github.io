Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long is a typical engagement?

Advisory projects typically run 4-12 weeks depending on scope and complexity. Coaching sessions are available as single sessions or monthly packages. I tailor every engagement to your specific needs, timeline, and budget constraints.

What industries do you work with?

Primarily automotive, aerospace, consumer goods, and industrial manufacturing. My frameworks apply across discrete and process manufacturing environments. I've worked with everything from 50-person facilities to Fortune 500 operations.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

Both! Most advisory work is remote with periodic on-site visits for kickoffs, training sessions, and key milestones. Coaching is 100% virtual via video call. I'm flexible and can adapt to what works best for your team.

What's your pricing model?

Project-based pricing for advisory work, hourly rates for coaching, and custom packages for ongoing partnerships. Every engagement starts with a free 15-minute discovery call to understand your needs and provide a transparent proposal.

What tools and platforms do you use?

Primary focus on Power BI for analytics, but I also work with Excel, SQL databases, ERP systems (SAP, Oracle), MES platforms, and various manufacturing execution systems. I'm tool-agnostic and focus on what delivers results for your specific environment.

How quickly can we see results?

Most clients see measurable improvements within 4-6 weeks. Quick wins (dashboard prototypes, metric definitions) often happen in weeks 1-2. Sustainable change and full adoption typically take 8-12 weeks depending on organizational readiness and complexity.
